CONTROL • LAUNCH • AUTOMATE

Everything. Connected.

HOOKS is a next-generation launcher that unifies apps, games, lights, and smart home tech into one seamless interface. Forget juggling multiple launchers, dashboards, or control panels - HOOKS brings everything together in a single menu designed for speed, clarity, and control.

It began as a small PowerShell script I wrote to fire webhooks for my sim rig and TV. Over time, with every new piece of gear and every feature request, it evolved into a full desktop application. Today, HOOKS is a complete ecosystem for launching, automating, and controlling your setup.

Who’s it for? Gamers, streamers, smart home enthusiasts, and creators. Whether you want a clutter-free way to launch your favorite games, a reliable panel to manage lighting, or smart automation that reacts to your presence - HOOKS is built to streamline the experience.
